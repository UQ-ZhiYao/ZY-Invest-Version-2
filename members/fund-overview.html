Fund Overview

The ZY-Invest fund mandate, objective, and key facts.

About the Fund

ZY-Invest is a founder-led private investment fund based in Malaysia, established in December 2021. The fund pools capital from a close network of family and associates and invests primarily in Bursa Malaysia equities, with selective US exposure.

Our objective is consistent, risk-aware capital growth across market cycles — measured by the daily Net Tangible Asset (NTA) value per unit. We favour quality businesses with durable earnings, held with patience and conviction.

The fund operates on MYR as its base currency and publishes a daily NTA per unit to every investor through this portal.
